


				Boolean identities



________ Commutative laws ______________________________________________________

(x AND y) = (y AND x)
(x OR y) = (y OR x)


________ Associative laws ______________________________________________________

(x AND (y AND z)) = ((x AND y) AND z)
(x OR (y OR z)) =((x OR y) OR z)


________ Distributive laws _____________________________________________________

(x AND (y OR z)) = (x AND y) OR (x AND z)
(x OR (y AND z)) = (x OR y) AND (x OR z)


________ De Morgan laws ________________________________________________________

NOT(x OR y) = NOT(x) AND NOT(y)
